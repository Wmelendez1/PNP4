Meet our Leader!

Hi I'm Merissa!

Merissa is an experienced team leader in the admissions department at Full Sail University. She is passionate about guiding prospective students in their educational journey, especially those interested in arts, technology, and media. With a strong background in digital media, Merissa brings a modern approach to her work. She values clear communication and teamwork, ensuring her team provides exceptional service to aspiring students. Outside of work, Merissa enjoys kayaking in local rivers and staying updated with the latest tech trends. Her dedication to mentorship and innovation makes her an invaluable asset to Full Sail University.
